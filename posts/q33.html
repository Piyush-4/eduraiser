Program to find area and perimeter of rectangle and area and circumference of circle
Eduraiser!

Problem:-

The length and breadth of a rectangle and radius of a circle are input through the keyboard. Write a program to calculate the area and perimeter of the rectangle, and the area and circumference of the circle.

To understand this program, you should be familiar with the following C programming topics:

  • C Input Output (I/O)
  • C Data Types
  • C Variables, Constants and Literals
  • C Operators

The program to find area and perimeter of a rectangle and area and circumference of a circle

                    
                        #include <stdio.h>
                        #define PI 3.14285 
                        int main() {
                        float l, b, rad, aor, per, aoc, cir;
                        printf("Enter the length and breadth of the rectangle:- ");
                        scanf("%f %f", &l, &b);
                        printf("\nEnter the Radius of the circle:- ");
                        scanf("%f", &rad);
                        //Finding area and perimeter of the rectangle and area and circumference of the circle
                        aor = l * b;
                        per = 2 * (l + b);
                        aoc = PI * rad * rad;
                        cir = 2 * PI * rad;
                        printf("\nArea of the Rectangle is:- %f", aor);
                        printf("\nPerimeter of the Rectangle is:- %f", per);
                        printf("\nArea of the circle is:- %f", aoc);
                        printf("\nCircumference of the circle is:- %f", cir);
                        return 0;
                        }
